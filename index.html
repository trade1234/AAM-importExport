    About Us

    Before establishing AAM Import & Export in 2024 G.C. the company was actively involved in logistics operations, specializing in transporting goods from Ethiopia to Djibouti since 2011 G.C. Founded by a team of experienced professionals, AAM Import & Export has since focused on building a strong reputation for excellence in the agricultural sector, leveraging its prior expertise in logistics to enhance its import and export capabilities.

    Company Activities

    AAM Import & Export specializes in the import and export trade of agricultural products. Managed by highly qualified professionals with extensive experience in international trade and the banking sector, the company exports Ethiopian soybeans, sesame seeds, pulses, cereals, and various oil seeds globally. Based in Addis Ababa, the capital city of Ethiopia, we are well-positioned to serve our international clientele.
